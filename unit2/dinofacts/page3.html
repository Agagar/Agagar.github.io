The Spinosaurus

SPINE-oh-SORE-us

Name meaning: 'Thorn Lizard'

Diet: carnivorous

Teeth: flat and blade-like

Food: fish and maybe other dinosaurs

How it moved: bipedal(on 2 legs)

When it lived: Late Cretaceous, 95-70 million years ago

Found in: Egypt & Morocco

Type of dinosaur: large theropod

Length: 14.0m

Weight: 7400kg

Spinosaurus is only known from fragmentary remains. Size estimates range up to 18m, which would make it the longest meat eater, although more slender than T. rex or Giganotosaurus.

its thought that Spinosaurus' snout contained pressure sensors like crocodiles, to help them hunt in the water.

Taxonomic details

Taxonomy: Dinosauria, Saurischia, Theropoda, Neotheropoda, Tetanurae, Spinosauroidea, Spinosauridae, Spinosaurinae

Named by: Stromer (1915)

Type species: aegyptiacus
